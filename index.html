Connect Four

The Classic Vertical Four-In-A-Row Checkers Game

How to Play

Summary

Connect Four is a two-player game board in which the players take turns dropping tokens from the top into a seven-column, six-row vertically suspended grid. The pieces fall straight down, occupying the next available space within the column. The objective of the game is to be the first to form an horizontal, vertical, or diagonal line of four of one's own token.

How to Play
  1. Decide who plays first!
  2. The first player will play black tokens, while the second with red tokens.
  3. Players will alternate turns after every move. Because is a 7 x 6 grid (42 available spaces), the maximum moves per player is 21.
  4. On your turn, go to the top of the grid, and click over the token corresponding to the desired column.
  5. The game automatically stops after one of the player's won (4 in a row), or if it's a tie.
  6. To play again, click 'Reset' and then press 'Start'. Good luck!
